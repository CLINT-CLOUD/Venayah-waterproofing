What We Do

Our Waterproofing
Services

Complete moisture protection across every surface type — residential, commercial, and industrial.

01
🏗️

Basement & Foundation

Complete below-grade waterproofing systems that prevent water ingress, protect structural integrity, and keep lower levels permanently dry.

  • Interior & exterior membrane systems
  • French drain installation
  • Crack injection & sealing
  • Sump pump systems
  • Rising damp treatment
02
🏛️

Roofing & Terraces

High-performance membranes for flat roofs, terraces, and podium decks designed for extreme weather and prolonged UV exposure.

  • Liquid applied membranes
  • Torch-on bitumen systems
  • Green roof waterproofing
  • Terrace deck coatings
  • Parapet wall treatment
03
🚿

Bathrooms & Wet Areas

Precision waterproofing for bathrooms, wet rooms, and all internal wet zones — lasting protection behind every tile and finish.

  • Shower & wet room tanking
  • Pool & spa waterproofing
  • Balcony & planter box sealing
  • Screed & tile bed preparation
  • Cementitious systems
04
🔍

Inspection & Assessment

Thorough waterproofing surveys and condition assessments to diagnose failures and recommend the most effective remediation.

  • Moisture meter surveys
  • Thermal imaging inspection
  • Detailed written reports
  • Remediation planning
  • Expert witness reporting

Not sure which service you need? Our certified team will visit your property and recommend the right solution at no cost.
